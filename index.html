jQTouch was created by David Kaneda as a means of easily creating iPhone-styled websites. It is released open source, under the MIT license. It is still in its early stages of development and is currently lacking in documentation. For more information about jQTouch, please contact David on Twitter, @davidkaneda.

Features

jQTouch
  • One-line setup, with options for selectors, viewport settings, icon path and glossiness, and status bar style
  • Pages can be built in a single HTML file, or loaded dynamically via GET or POST
  • Native, hardware-accelerated, page animations, including slide in/out, slide up/down, and 3D flip. All with history support.
  • Image preloading functions
  • Easy to theme
